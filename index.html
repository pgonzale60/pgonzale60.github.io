About Me

I'm Pablo, a bioinformatician currently maintaining production pharmacogenomics workflows at Oxford Nanopore Technologies. With a PhD from Cambridge and 8 peer-reviewed publications in journals including Nature Communications and PLoS Genetics, I specialize in building robust, reproducible pipelines for genomic analysis using Python, R, Nextflow, and modern workflow automation.

I've worked across the full stack: from quick Shiny apps like vis_ALG (built in under 2 hours and still used by researchers 5+ years later) to production Nextflow pipelines with automated testing and CI/CD. My approach combines rigorous scientific methodology with software engineering best practices—version control, containerization, and comprehensive documentation.

When I'm not building genomics tools, I explore diverse applications of data analysis: network representations for interactive storytelling, algorithmic trading strategies, and AI-assisted workflows. I bring clarity to complex situations, ensuring teams understand goals and action items.
